如图,易知 $h=\sqrt{3^2-1^2}=2\sqrt{2},$\\
$r^2+AE^2=(2\sqrt{2}-r)^2$\\
$r^2+2^2=r^2+8-4\sqrt{2}r$\\
$r=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$\\
$V=\frac{4\pi r^3}{3}=\frac{4}{3}\times  \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\times\frac{1}{2} \pi=\frac{\sqrt{2}\pi}{3}$\\
